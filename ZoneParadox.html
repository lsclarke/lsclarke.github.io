Zone Paradox

Zone Paradox is a simple puzzle action game that encourages the player to think outside the box as they shift between worlds in order to solve small puzzles to reach the overall end goal! I made this for the GameDev.tv Game Jam May 2023! But hope to keep working on it a bit more after some feedback!!

Time frame: 6 days
Solo Project
Game Engine: Unity Engine
Programming Language: C#
